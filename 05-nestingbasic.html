What is FOIA/FOIL: A "definition list"

Federal FOIA and FOIL (state) laws
Are the laws that protect your right to request information from federal, state and city agencies
FOIA
Applies to federal executive branch agencies. Does not apply to any other level of government
FOIL (state)
Not all FOIL are born equal. Every State has its own law
Exceptions:
  • FOIA: national security, internal agency rules, statutory exemption, trade secrets (sometimes an excuse to not give you database model layouts), inter-agency or intra-agency memos, personal privacy, some law enforcement records and other.
  • FOIL in NY State (each state is it’s own thing): similar to FOIA in 11 categories, including contract awards or collective bargaining negotiations
