Embedding Media from other websites

You have seen stories and webpages that include Tweets, Youtube videos, Gifs, Charts, Documents, etc. from these platforms and third party websites. They allow you to "embedd their content" in your webpage.

The tag used for this is <iframe> which stands for interactive frame. Like the Russian doll inside another Russian doll, it allows you to put a webpage inside your webpage

To make it easier most platforms provide you with a code snippet ready to be copied and pasted in your HTML code.

Tweets

Go to the Tweet you want to embed. Click on the upper right arrow, then click on Embed Tweet and a box with a code snippet will show up. Copy it and paste it in your HTML code. Result:
